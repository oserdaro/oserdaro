Designing a Spreadsheet-based LMS: Splemasy

Bochumer Bildungszentrum (BBZ), one of the major education institutes in Bochum, Germany participated in a government initiative called "Extra Zeit zum Lernen" to help ease the effects of Corona Pandemie on school children providing them after school remedial classes. At BBZ the project was realized between Oktober 2020 and August 2023 with around 1400 students and 160 teachers. Total cost of the project was around 1.5 million Euros.

For planning and smooth functioning of the classes as well as the financial management I designed an online collaborative learning system called "Splemasy" based on Google Sheets.

SPLEMASY is an acronym for Spreadsheet-based Leaarning Management System.

With Splemasy the management of BBZ was able to register new students and teachers, make gropus, assign groups to teacehrs, record class contents, get lesson reports, calculate teacher pays and prepare reports to submit the authorities.
