About me

My passion for Information Technology started when I went to a vocational high school to study Electronics. I learned the basics of analog and digital electronics. After graduation I went to a vocational college and studied Electronics further. That was the time I first met with a computer.

Right after university graduation I was offered a job in India to lead an educational project and I accepted the offer. I attended a software course and learned Visual Basic and was awarded the 'Web Application Developer Certificate'.

I worked in Ukraine and learned SQL. I worked in Bangladesh and Macedonia and used my IT-skills in various projects.

I came to Germany and learned Google AppsScript. I worked in Bochumer Bildungszentrum as Data Manager. I used JavaScript and Google Cloud Technologies to manage two different educational projects which involved hundreds of students, tens of teachers and a budget of around 1,5 million Euros. This is the time when I fell in love with Data Science.

I worked as English and IT teacher at a 'Berufskolleg'. I taught Database Modelling with Visio and MySQL.

I decided to delve deeper. I enrolled in a Data Science course at Clarusway Academy. It is a full-time (Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 16:45) 10-month course (August-June 2023). I am currently learning Data Analysis with Python, Data Visualization, Machine Learning and Deep Learning.
