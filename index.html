About me

I was born in Turkey. My passion for Information Technology started when I earned a Technical High School Diploma in Electronics. I learned the basics of Analog & Digital Electronics. At university I completed a BA in English. I bought my first PC. I learned MS Excel and used it for my dissertation.

In India, I worked as a school director. I attended a software course and learned Visual Basic and MS ASP and designed the school's website.

In Ukraine, I worked as English teacher and magazine editor. I learned Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign. I designed a monthly bilingual magazine called "Meridian". I learned VBA, MS Access and SQL and created a database for library management.

In Bangladesh, I worked as head of English department. Simultaneously, I earned my MA in Linguistics and ELT. I studied human languages and became more interested in computer languages. I developed a student testing program called "FineTesting" using MS Access, SQL and VBA.

In Macedonia, I worked as head of English and Project Departments. At the same time, I completed my PhD in Language Learning Motivation. In my research I used various methodologies and SPSS for statistical analysis. I developed an LMS with Excel and VBA and a project management system with MS Access.

I came to Germany 6 years ago and learned German.

In Germany I worked as a data manager for an educational institute called "Bochumer Bildungszentrum". I learned Google Sheets and Google AppsScript and developed an online LMS called "LernSmart" and "Splemasy". Using these applications I successfully managed two large projects involving hundreds of students, tens of teachers and a budget of about 1.5 million Euros. It was during this time when I fell in love with Data Science.

I worked as English and IT teacher at a "Berufskolleg". I taught Database Modelling with MS Visio and MySQL. My interest and focus on Data Science grew. I decided to delve deeper into Data Science and make a career change.

I completed a Further Education & Training in Data Science and Analytics. It was 1480 hours, 10 months full-time course, from August 2023 to June 2024. I learned Python and its popular libraries for Data Cleaning, Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, Deployment, as well as Tableau, MS SQL, Advanced Google Sheets, and GIT, and finished many projects.

Now I am a proud Data Scientist.
